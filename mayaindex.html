maya - boba tea bases

in this webpage, we'll inform you of various bases for your boba tea.

popular boba tea bases:

  1. milk tea
  2. fruit tea
  3. matcha tea
  4. taro tea

why is the base important?

the base of your boba tea is crucial as it sets the flavor profile (in essence, it makes your tea unique). here are some common flavor profiles:

  • milk tea - creamy and rich
  • fruit tea - refreshing and fruity
  • matcha tea - earthy and vibrant
  • taro tea - nutty and sweet
